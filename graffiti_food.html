GRAFFITI FOOD

Graffiti Food is a mobile application built by Augmented Reality. This app can visualize the reviews on the wall of buildings. People can see the reviews of nearby restaurants through the camera as well as post the reviews for the businesses. it gives users a more direct way to get to know the restaurants nearby.
