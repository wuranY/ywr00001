LUMIE

Lumie is a light-sensitive lamp that can automatically turn on or off depending on whether there is movement in specific area. It helps people to get rid of the use of traditional physical switch and meet the need of saving energy for users. By using the mobile application users can control and operate multiple devices on their phone.
