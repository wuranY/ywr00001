YELP REDESIGN

Yelp is a website to help people to explore the business nearby. It mainly provides comprehensive information of restaurant to people such as menu, review and photo. Yelp has been adding new features to enrich the convenience and experience for users, but I think there is still UX that can be improved.

This project is to redesign three pages on the website of Yelp depending on the researches of target audience analysis, personas, problem identifications etc. and then create hide fidelity wireframes for both desktop and mobile platforms.
